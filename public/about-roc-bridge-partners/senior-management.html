Senior Management

Our principals have an average of 21 years’ experience and have extensive experience in all facets of real estate investment, financing, development, management and operations, spanning all major assets classes including multifamily apartment and condominium, hotel, industrial, office, single-family residential retail and storage.

With over 500 real estate professionals in over 40 unique submarkets across the U.S., ROC|Bridge has a pool of resources that is well versed in local markets throughout the country.
