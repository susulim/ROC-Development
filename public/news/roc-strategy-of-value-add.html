ROC Strategy for Value Add:

  • Implement comprehensive capital improvement strategy consisting of significiant improvements to common areas, lobby, amenities and parking.
  • Install new property management and leasing teams to operate the property and vitalize leasing efforts.
  • Work with local partners. affiliates and city officials to reposition the development site as a high density multifamily residential use. (Directly adjacent to this property is a very high end residential tower with over 100 units per acre density, currently 95% leased. ROC has received initial interest from credible prospective buyers to buy this property for development as a high end residential property.
  • Market the Campus buildings for sale, once development and parking structure are approved.
  • Use the sale proceeds from the Campus and/or the development land to construct a parking structure to add a covered parking element to the Corporate Center while maintaining the 4+ per 1,000 parking ratio.
  • Lease the Corporate Center to 90%+ (stabilized occupancy) by the 3rd year, and will look to exit this final Tower of the Cherry Creek complex in year 4 to 5.
