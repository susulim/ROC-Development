Torrey Ridge Apartments – Fresno, CA

Units: 418
Acquisition Date: June 23, 2010
Strategy:
  • Raise Revenues through common area & amenity improvements and implementing a utility reimbursement program
  • Reduce expenses through better on-site management controls
Status: Unrealized – Stabilized

Oppurtunity

ROC|Bridge was presented with the opportunity to purchase this GE lender owned multifamily asset at approximately 60% of replacement cost. The property consists of 70 two-story buildings with a large average unit sizes. The property included a decommissioned executive golf course which ROC|Bridge capitalized on by adding several features to make the asset the best amenitized asset in its submarket.

The opportunity in this transaction was acquiring an excellent physical asset in a good neighborhood with the ability to increase net operating income through substantial expense savings and raising rents by implementing the improvements described above.

Execution

ROC|Bridge's principals purchased the asset in June 2010. Since acquisition, ROC|Bridge has completed the following:

  • $1.5 million in capital improvements – the improvements consisted of security upgrades, clubhouse renovations, playgrounds, soccer fields, extensive landscaping, Frisbee golf course, walking path, and certain deferred maintenance improvements.
  • A utility reimbursement program which increased revenues substantially.
  • Reduced operating expenses by renegotiating utility and other contracts.
  • Increased annualized net operating income from approximately $1.6 million to about $2.1 million.

Results

ROC|Bridge has completed the business plan for this property and have put it under contract to sell ahead of schedule and at a higher price than underwritten. The sale is expected to be completed by the 2nd half of 2012 at a higher internal rate of return than originally forecasted.
