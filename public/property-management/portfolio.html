Current Portfolio

BPM manages over 16,500 multifamily apartment units and over 1.5 million square feet of commercial office space. It manages (i) most of the properties owned by ROC|Bridge’s privately held real estate investment vehicles, and (ii) properties owned by other funds, institutional capital aggregators and individual investors.

Our property management division employs over 500 real estate professionals, in over 40 unique sub-markets across the U.S. This includes 54 property managers and 120 leasing agents, and over 320 on-site personnel providing property maintenance and operations.
