Current Portfolio

BPM manages over 16,500 multifamily apartment units and over 1.5 million square feet of commercial office space, which are owned by ROC|Bridge’s privately held real estate investment vehicles, other funds, institutional capital aggregators and individual investors.

Our property management division employs over 550 real estate professionals, in over 40 unique sub-markets across the U.S. This includes 54 property managers and 120 leasing agents, and over 320 on-site personnel providing property maintenance and operations.
